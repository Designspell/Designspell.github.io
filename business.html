Company Discription:

Collab is a tech platform which enhances employee engagement through social learning and skill sharing. Collab uses a matchmaking algorithm which enables employees of the organization to connect with peers to learn new skills and knowledge, where they can come share their experiential learnings and best practices. (Who in my organization is expert in Excel and can help me with a particular problem. Who in my organization has excellent English skills – I need to check if my translation makes sense)

Company Discription:

Work Pack has built project management software for Engineering and Project Teams in the field of construction and renewable energies. They service customers where Excel and Word Docs are no longer sufficient for Project Management but SAP Solutions are not yet feasible or too expensive. With their software you can:  Upload and Manage Documents  Share and manage Access Rights  Comment and Mark Up  Comment Resolution and Approval and  Track Progress Besides their Product Work Pack, their solution „Cubicle“ is a complete project management platform with different modules for Scheduling, Progress Tracking, Cost Tracking, Resource Management & Performance Monitoring. WorkPack and Cubicle are easily integrated to offer an end to end complete work management solution for project intensive companies – Engineering Consultants, EPC, Contractors, Construction Managers and Project Management Consultants.

http://www.workpack.in/

Company Discription:

Quovid has built a collaborative Video Platform that enables businesses to set up customized Streaming Platforms for corporate video streaming. The technology has been used in Tele-Medicine, E-Learning and Training and Customer engagement. PWC India has choosen Quovid to set up a “Virtual Classroom” to enhance employee training. As of today, Quovid has delivered 700.000 hours of Streaming within 19 Months and aims to specialize in the Tele Medicine Market.

https://www.quovid.com/

Company Discription:

Quick Metris is a Social Media Management Platform that delivers a deep insight on Brand Performance across all Social Media Channels. Consumer oriented brands typically leverage Digital/Social media for understanding customers, how the category is evolving, even brand building and customer service. QuickMetrix not only does this, but also goes a step further and connects Brands with real customers and prospects on social media. This provides a unified experience to customers for 2-way interactions with the enterprise (complaint, new sale or plain engagement). It is an Enterprise Social Media Automation product that allows end-to-end functions of Listening, Engagement, Publishing and Analysis. The information is real time for better insights and helps drive customer engagement.

http://www.quickmetrix.com/
