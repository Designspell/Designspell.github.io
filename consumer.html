A recent study by the McKinsey Global Institute (MGI) suggests that if India continues to grow at the current pace, average household incomes will triple over the next two decades, making the country the world’s fifth-largest consumer economy by 2025. Already in 2020, India's share of global consumption would expand to 5.8 per cent – a gigantic market is emerging. India's
e-commerce market alone is likely to reach US$ 38 billion in 2016.15; the online retail sector in India is expected to be a US$ 1 trillion market by 2020. Amazon expects India to become its quickest market to reach US$ 10 billion in gross merchandise value (GMV) and to become its largest overseas market surpassing Japan, Germany and the UK.
