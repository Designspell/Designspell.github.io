The Venture Capital Arm of Bombay Ventures Scouts for promising Technologies in close cooperation with Austrian Industry and StartUps and locks in cooperations by placing strategic Co-Investments with a ticket size ranging from EUR 50.000 to EUR 250.000. With 6000+ Startups founded every year, looking for suitable candidates is like looking for a needle in a needle-stack. Success of finding a needle is almost certain but the process might not be without perils. A big advantage of the Indian market is that ideas and talents are concentrated in Delhi, Mumbai and Bangalore. In these cities, numerous accelerators and co-working spaces have been set up by investors, universities and large cooperations alike. These spaces act as efficient filters in the scouting process and the need for active marketing is negligible. The interest of these players to cooperate with a European fund is very high for reasons discussed in the previous chapters.
